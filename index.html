What types of website services do you offer?
I offer custom websites that are, most preferrably, built using JAMstack, but I can built it for you using a framework if you really need to use one.
How long does it take to complete a website or discord server project?
This is usually not possible to tell how long a project can take before considering the specific details and requirements. It could take anywhere from few days to weeks, please contact me to get a best estimate for your project.
How do you work with clients? What is your process?
All of the communication happens through fiverr or telegram chat. Before starting a project, I will explain to you how will each of your requests will be delivered by me including but not limited to costs, resources, hosting, etc. And we will sign a Statement of Work (SOW) that will list all the agreed upon deliverables, scope of the project, expected time of completion, milestones and payment methods. And I will be in constant touch with you at each step of the project. I will personally make sure you are satisfied with my work.

At the moment, I accept payment through fiverr (for International clients) and UPI (for Indian Clients).
